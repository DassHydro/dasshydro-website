What is HiVDI ?

HiVDI = Hybrid Hierarchical Variational Discharge Inference

The HiVDI algorithm aims at estimating rivers discharge and rivers bathymetry from measurements of the SWOT instrument (NASA-CNES et al. mission).
In a nutshell, the algorithm relies on DassFlow1D code , Deep Learning and a Bayesian analysis, see figure below (details available in [Larnier-Monnier 2023]).

The algorithm is here available upon simple request.

SWOT measurements

SWOT instrument is an altimeter providing Water Surface Elevation (WSE) and river width measurements at ~250 m scale (with an accuracy +/-10cm for WSE). Each overpass provides two swaths of 60 km width each at ~10-20 days frequency depending on the location on Earth.
